About

Technical events play a major role in shaping the minds of Engineering students and giving them a great platform.
We have been providing a lot of great opportunities for the students from gvpce,we felt now can be the right time to have an inter-college Ideathon.

The topic we have chosen for this year's Ideathon is "Reinventing Imagination".We are hoping to receive ideas that are realistic and also create a warping change and leaves an impact.

We aim to break boundaries and the technical stigma that students assume.
